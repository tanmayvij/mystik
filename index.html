Vastu Shastra

Vastu shastra is a traditional Indian system of architecture originating in India. Vastu Shastra incorporates traditional Hindu and (in some cases) Buddhist beliefs. The designs aim to integrate architecture with nature, the relative functions of various parts of the structure, and ancient beliefs utilising geometric patterns (yantra), symmetry, and directional alignments.
